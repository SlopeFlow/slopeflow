# SlopeFlow Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 1, 2026

## Overview
SlopeFlow is a trading education app for teenagers. We collect minimal data and never share it with third parties.

## Data We Collect
- Email address (for account creation)
- Age (to verify eligibility)
- App usage data (streaks, watchlist, challenge results)

## How We Use Data
- Authenticate your account
- Track your learning progress
- Improve the app experience

## Data Storage
All data is stored securely via Supabase. We use industry-standard encryption.

## Third-Party Services
- Supabase (authentication and database)
- CoinGecko (cryptocurrency data)
- Yahoo Finance (stock data)

## Your Rights
You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through the app settings.
