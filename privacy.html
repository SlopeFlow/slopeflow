Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 1, 2026

Overview

SlopeFlow is a trading education app for teenagers. We collect minimal data and never share it with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Data We Collect

  • Email address (for account creation)
  • Age (to verify eligibility)
  • App usage data (streaks, watchlist, challenge results)

How We Use Your Data

  • Authenticate your account
  • Track your learning progress and streaks
  • Improve the app experience

Data Storage

All data is stored securely via Supabase using industry-standard encryption. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — authentication and database
  • CoinGecko — cryptocurrency market data
  • Yahoo Finance — stock market data
  • RSS Feeds — financial news (CoinDesk, CoinTelegraph)

Children's Privacy

SlopeFlow is designed for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Your Rights

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through the app settings. Upon deletion, all personal data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.
